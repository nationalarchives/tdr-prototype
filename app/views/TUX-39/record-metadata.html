Record metadata

During upload we extract the following metadata from your records:

  • File name
  • File path
  • Checksum
  • Date Last Modified

Read more about extracted metadata.

As part of most digital transfers to The National Archives there is additional metadata that you or your department need to provide. This helps us to preserve your records.
