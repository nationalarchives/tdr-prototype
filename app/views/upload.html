Upload your records

Before uploading, all records (files and folders) you wish to transfer must be put into a single, top-level folder.

There is a size limit of 2GB for an individual file and we ask that you upload no more than 500 files per consignment. If you need to transfer larger files, contact us at tdr@nationalarchives.gov.uk. If your folder contains files that we cannot accept, you may have to start again.

Check which files we accept
We cannot accept files and folders which are password protected, zipped or contain slashes (/ and \) in the name. You must remove all thumbnail images (thumbs.db) and executable files (.exe). Empty folders will not be transferred.
