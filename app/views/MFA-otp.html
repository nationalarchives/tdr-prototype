Where do I find the one-time passcode?
  • The code can be accessed using the authenticator app on your smart device that you used when you registered for an account. The app is likely to be called "Authenticator".
  • Open the app on your device and go to the Transfer Digital Records account within the app to retrieve the 6 digit one time passcode
  • If the code doesn’t work, wait for the app to refresh and generate a new code and try using that
  • Contact tdr@nationalarchives.gov.uk if you need support
