Before you start a transfer

We can only accept a single document in Microsoft Word (docx) format.

The document must contain the following information:

  • neutral citation
  • name(s) of judge(s)
  • name(s) of parties
  • court and judgment date

If this is an amendment to a previous transfer

You can use this service to transfer an update or revision to a previous transfer.

Once you have successfully transferred the amended document notify us by email (judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk) and include the following:

  • the neutral citation
  • the consignment reference provided at the end of this transfer
  • tell us if this transfer is a revision or update

Other reasons you may need to contact us

Once the transfer is complete, you should also notify us by email (judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk) if you need to:

  • Flag that your judgment is subject to an anonymisation order
  • Send supplementary material for this judgment

Make sure you quote the consigment reference number provided on the last step of the transfer and attach any supplementary material to the email.
