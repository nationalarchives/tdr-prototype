Before you start a transfer

What your document must contain

We can only accept a single document in Microsoft Word (docx) format. It must contain the following information:

  • neutral citation
  • name(s) of judge(s)
  • name(s) of parties
  • court and judgment date

How to format your document

After you submit a Microsoft Word document we convert the content into a web page and PDF. To ensure the information is converted correctly, we ask you to follow some formatting guidelines and rules in the original document.

Read our detailed guidance on formatting your document.

The web page view may look slightly different to the original document but will contain the same information. This is to make sure the document meets basic web accessibility standards. The pdf will be as close a copy of the original document as possible. If you have any questions about document formatting, email judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk.

If this is an update or revision

You can use this service to transfer an update or revision.

Transfer the document in the same way as any judgement or decision, by clicking "Start transfer" above.

Once you have successfully completed the transfer email judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk, and include the following:

  • the neutral citation
  • the consignment reference provided at the end of this transfer
  • tell us if this transfer is a revision or update

Other reasons you may need to contact us

Once the transfer is complete, you should email judgments@nationalarchives.gov.uk if you need to:

  • Flag that your judgment is subject to an anonymisation order
  • Send supplementary material for this judgment

Make sure you quote the consigment reference number provided on the last step of the transfer and attach any supplementary material to the email.
